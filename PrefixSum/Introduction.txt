**Prefix Sum** is a very important technique in **DSA (Arrays, Hashing, Sliding Window, Matrix)**. Many problems use it to compute **range sums, subarrays, and cumulative values efficiently**.

Below is a **categorized list of common Prefix Sum problems** that are frequently asked in coding interviews and platforms like **LeetCode**, **GeeksforGeeks**, and **Codeforces**.
